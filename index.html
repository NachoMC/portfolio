ComoPicaro Roguelike

University project

It's a Roguelike game. The character goes through the dungeon rooms, where she will have to fight numerous enemies with her bow.

She has a ultimate ability that charges gradually with each successful shot. Also, there are different types of power ups that the player receives when she clears all the enemies of each room.

The enemies and rooms environment are generated procedurally with progressively increasing difficulty and loaded in runtime using level streaming. There are multiple types of enemies with different behaviors, different types of projectiles and a final boss.
